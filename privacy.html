Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security matter to us

Introduction

Welcome to CRS Conclave 2.0. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information when you register for and participate in our event. By submitting your registration, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for CRS Conclave 2.0, we collect the following information:

Team Information:

  • Team name
  • Number of team members

School Information:

  • School name
  • Supervising teacher's name, email, and phone number

Participant Information:

  • Full name of each team member
  • Grade/class level
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers

Technical Information:

  • Registration timestamp and date
  • Browser type and device information (user agent)
  • IP address (automatically collected)
  • Page referral information

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • Event Management: To process registrations, manage participant records, and organize event logistics
  • Communication: To send event updates, schedules, important announcements, and emergency notifications
  • Verification: To verify eligibility and prevent duplicate registrations
  • Safety and Security: To ensure the safety of participants and maintain event security
  • Competition Administration: To manage team assignments, scoring, and results
  • Certificates and Recognition: To create and distribute participation certificates and awards
  • Future Events: To inform you about future CRS Conclave events (you may opt out)
  • Analysis and Improvement: To analyze participation trends and improve future events

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it:

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), which uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols
  • Access Control: Only authorized organizers and administrators have access to participant data
  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL protocols
  • Regular Backups: We maintain secure backups to prevent data loss
  • Limited Retention: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for event purposes

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • School Authorities: With supervising teachers and school administrators for coordination purposes
  • Event Partners: With sponsors, judges, and venue partners as necessary for event operations (limited to essential information only)
  • Public Recognition: Team names and school names may be displayed publicly on scoreboards, certificates, and promotional materials
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety
  • With Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

We will never share: Individual contact details (email addresses and phone numbers) with third parties without explicit consent.

5. Photography and Media

During CRS Conclave 2.0, we may capture photographs, videos, and recordings of event activities. By participating, you consent to:

  • Being photographed or recorded during event activities
  • Use of such media for promotional, educational, and archival purposes
  • Publication on official websites, social media, and marketing materials

If you do not wish to be photographed or recorded, please inform event organizers in advance, and we will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your request.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our registration website may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and registration process
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website and improve user experience
  • Firebase Analytics: To monitor registration system performance and troubleshoot issues

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

CRS Conclave 2.0 is designed for students in grades 9-12, many of whom may be under 18 years of age. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We require parental/guardian consent for participants under 18
  • We collect only information necessary for event participation
  • Teacher supervision is mandatory for all teams
  • We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from minors
  • We implement age-appropriate privacy protections

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications while still receiving essential event updates
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw your registration and consent (may result in inability to participate)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: ibcpudgam@gmail.com

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for different periods depending on the purpose:

  • Registration Data: Retained for the duration of the event and up to 2 years afterward for records and future event planning
  • Communication Records: Retained for 1 year after the event
  • Photos and Media: Retained indefinitely for archival and promotional purposes (with option to request removal)
  • Anonymous Statistics: May be retained indefinitely for analysis

After retention periods expire, personal information is securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your information:

  • Firebase (Google): For database storage and authentication
  • Email Service Providers: For sending event communications
  • Cloud Hosting: For website hosting and data storage

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We choose providers that maintain high data protection standards.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By registering, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that adequate safeguards are in place when transferring data internationally.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify registered participants of significant changes via email. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom indicates when the policy was last revised.

Continued participation after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

Email: ibcpudgam@gmail.com

We will respond to your requests within 30 days.

14. Consent

By checking the consent box during registration, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understood this Privacy Policy
  • You consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described
  • You have obtained necessary parental/guardian consent if under 18
  • You agree to receive essential event communications
  • You understand your rights and how to exercise them
