What is CRS Conclave?

CRS Conclave 2.0 is Udgam's premier interschool technology and business event, bringing together the brightest young minds from across the region. This two-day extravaganza combines cutting-edge technology workshop, entrepreneurship challenges, and innovative competitions.

Our event serves as a launchpad for students to explore emerging technologies, develop business acumen, and network with the cohort, creating an ecosystem where innovation thrives.

Our Purpose

The primary purpose of CRS Conclave 2.0 is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application. We aim to create a platform where students can transform their innovative ideas into tangible projects and solutions.

Through hands-on workshops, competitive challenges, and collaborative activities, we inspire students to think critically, work creatively, and develop the entrepreneurial mindset necessary to thrive in tomorrow's digital economy.

Our goal is to nurture a community of young innovators who are not just consumers of technology, but creators and leaders who will shape the future of business and technology.
